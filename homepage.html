BOW-WOW

About Us

BOW-WOW is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting animal welfare in the communities of our city and its neighboring areas.

Our mission revolves around rescuing and rehabilitating injured, needy, and sick animals, ensuring they receive the care and support they deserve.

We are committed to being the voice for the voiceless, advocating for the rights and well-being of all animals.

BOW-WOW has been actively involved in various initiatives aimed at improving the lives of animals in our region.
